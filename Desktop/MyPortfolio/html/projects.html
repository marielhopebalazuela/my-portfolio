D-TECT System

D-TECT is our undergraduate thesis project, a machine learning system designed to predict dengue risk severity in San Pablo City. Our group analyzed a dataset of over 243,265 records to identify outbreak trends. My part was training and comparing various models including LSTM, XGBoost, GBM, Random Forest, Decision Tree, and SVM to ensure the highest prediction accuracy. I was also in charge of creating the system flowcharts and drafting the majority of the project documentation.

Python & ML Supabase Seaborn & Matplotlib

BurnItNow

BurnItNow is a web application that helps users track their fitness progress. It uses a machine learning model trained with XGBoost to accurately predict how many calories a user burns based on the intensity and duration of their exercises. The goal is to provide users with clear, data-driven insights to help them reach their fitness goals more effectively.

Web App Data Analytics Health Tech

BookNook PH

BookNook PH was developed as part of my internship training at Armada Logics Inc. It is an e-commerce platform designed to make buying and selling pre-loved books easier. My contributions included designing low-fidelity wireframes and building the front-end architecture to ensure a secure and smooth user experience during login and transactions.
